About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary designer and motion artist based in Bengaluru. My work blends visual storytelling, playful aesthetics and technical experimentation. Inspired by vintage technology and the disco era, I craft immersive experiences that make products dance and ideas shine.

When I’m not building interactive websites, you can find me exploring new 3D tools, listening to Italo‑disco or experimenting with generative art. Let’s collaborate and create something extraordinary together.
